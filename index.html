1. Introduction

Logic Grid Master is an Android logic puzzle game developed by 0xPriya (Independent Developer). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the app, how it is used, and the rights you have regarding that information.

The purpose of this policy is to be fully transparent about our data practices and to comply with Google Play Store policies, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

By installing or using Logic Grid Master, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

🔒 Short version: We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information. No account is required to play.
📋

3. How We Use Information

  • App functionality — Save puzzle progress, XP, streaks, and achievements locally on your device
  • Crash diagnostics — Diagnose and fix bugs using anonymised Crashlytics reports
  • Analytics — Understand which features players use most to guide future improvements
  • Personalisation — Adapt puzzle difficulty to your skill level using aggregated play statistics (processed locally on device)
  • Advertising — Display relevant ads via Google AdMob to support the free version of the app
  • In-app review — Trigger the Play Store review prompt at appropriate milestones (3rd, 10th, 25th puzzle solved)

We never sell, rent, trade, or share your data with third parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes.

5. Advertising

The free version of Logic Grid Master displays ads served by Google AdMob. Ads help us offer the app at no cost.

Ad Type Where It Appears Mandatory?
Banner Ad Home screen and puzzle list Yes (free version)
Interstitial Ad After puzzle completion Yes (free version)
Rewarded Ad Hint preview dialog — player's choice No — fully optional

AdMob may use your device's Google Advertising ID (GAID) to serve personalised ads based on your interests.

To opt out of personalised ads:
Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation

To reset your Advertising ID:
Android Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID

💎 Purchasing the Premium upgrade removes all ads permanently. No ad SDK calls are made once Premium is active.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade any user data to third parties. Data is only shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Third-party SDKs — Firebase and AdMob receive anonymised technical data as described in Section 4, solely for the purposes stated

  • All network communication uses TLS (HTTPS) — no data is transmitted in plain text

11. International Users & GDPR

Logic Grid Master is available globally. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the following additional rights apply to you under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal basis for processing — Data is processed on the basis of legitimate interests (crash reporting, analytics) and consent (personalised ads via AdMob)
  • Right to object — You may object to processing based on legitimate interests at any time
  • Right to restriction — You may request that we restrict processing of your data
  • Right to lodge a complaint — You may lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority (DPA)
  • Data transfers — Firebase and AdMob data is processed on Google Cloud servers, which operate under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission

For California residents: we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell personal information. You may exercise your rights by contacting us at the email below.
